Let me introduce myself...

About seven years ago, I was working as an office manager for a small company. On my first day, I was given the task of building the company website. I was pumped!

I had never done this before, but always thought it was the coolest skill to have. I started learning with a template. Before I knew it, I was customizing with shortcode. And I was hooked!

Web developement became an obvious career change for me. There is always new languages and techniques to learn. Plus, there is this amazing community of developers that collaborate in support of making the industry better.

If you want to learn more about me, you can see my currrent projects, services I offer, and my contact information below.

More About Me

For the last 20 years, I have worked as a bookkeeper and office manager for small companies. My experience has been in a range of industries including software, self-storage, HVAC & plumbing, hospitality and architecture. This broad experience has allowed me to develop a diverse skill set, adaptability, and an objective professional prespective.

Offering web development services will be a great addition to my skill set. It allows me to be creative with the user in mind. I will be able to help small business drive business to their website and streamline the process of gathering market information and utilizing that data to better serve the customer.
